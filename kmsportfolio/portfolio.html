Great Basin Fiber Arts Fair Website

From 2014 to 2018, I volunteered with the Great Basin Fiber Arts Fair. I headed up the classes section of the fair and increased class attendance by 100%. I helped to design this page of the website which was written using Wix.

Garden Dynamics Website

I attended the Lehi Spring Cohort of Tech Moms in 2022. This is an 8 week coding bootcamp where we learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and were introduced to careers in tech. Our graduation project was to create and present a website. I designed and wrote this website from scratch for my brother-in-law's business, Garden Dynamics. I also created his logo. The form on the Availability page was written so that his customers could contact him, but so that his information is not viewable online. I had to teach myself PHP in a week to be able to add the form to his website.

American Fork Family Search Center Website

I volunteered as the Webmaster for the American Fork Family Search Center in early 2023. The website was created using Google Sites and was mostly complete when I took over the project. I designed the footer and much of the Consultants Resources page. I also learned OnceHub to manage the scheduling of appointments for the site. I will maintain and update the site going forward.
